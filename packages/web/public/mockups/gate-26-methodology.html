Methodology

How the engine works, and how it cites.

You give a situation. The engine screens it against published rules and returns a ranked, source-cited shortlist of countries, regions, and towns. Every figure is shown with its source, verified date, and confidence.

Note

Sourced screening, not legal, tax, or financial advice; verify with a professional.

The method

From your situation to a cited shortlist.

01

Situation

You describe your situation: income, household, work status, must-haves, and dealbreakers. Nothing here is advice; it is the input to a screen.

02

Screen against published rules

Each candidate place is scored across seven dimensions, cost of living, tax, visa, residency, climate, healthcare, and safety, using published rules and figures. Scores are computed only on the dimensions you weighted, then renormalised to those dimensions, so a place is never penalised for a dimension you chose to ignore.

03

Ranked, cited shortlist

The output is a ranked shortlist of countries, regions, and towns. Each ranking is traceable to the figures behind it, and each figure carries its source, verified date, and confidence.

Dealbreakers are applied as a separate filter, not as a score. A place that fails a dealbreaker is removed from the shortlist before ranking; it is never quietly outweighed.

Confidence

What the confidence marks mean.

High

Primary source, verified within the last six months.

Medium

Dated, or drawn from a secondary source.

Low

Modeled, or not yet verified against a primary source. Low is calm, not a warning; it is never shown in red.

Freshness

A figure is never quietly stale.

Figures are re-checked on a schedule. Each one is treated as stale after a set window past its verified date. When a figure passes that window, a staleness banner is shown, not hidden. The engine never silently serves a stale number.

Past refresh

The fence

It screens. It does not advise.

The engine reports published rules and the figures behind them. It does not recommend a country, a regime, or a move, and it takes no affiliate position on what it screens. Anything consequential is routed to a licensed professional, behind this line.
