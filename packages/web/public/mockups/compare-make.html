Greece vs Portugal vs Spain

Six decisions — cost, tax, residency, climate, healthcare, and safety — with every figure sourced, dated, and confidence-rated.

🇬🇷 Greece recommended

Greece wins on tax. At 7% flat for qualifying retirees and €250k Golden Visa entry, it offers the most cost-effective path to Mediterranean residency. Portugal's IFICI (20%) and Spain's Beckham Law (24%) are competitive but materially more expensive.

🇬🇷 Greece recommended

Athens costs €600/mo less than Madrid and has the lowest Golden Visa entry point. Portugal's D7 passive-income visa is an accessible alternative. Spain lags on entry cost but leads on sunshine and healthcare infrastructure.

🇵🇹 Portugal recommended

Portugal earns it on safety — ranked 7th in the world — backed by solid healthcare (68/100) and the IFICI tax regime. Greece undercuts it on cost and tax. Spain leads on healthcare alone. Portugal is the most balanced choice.
