Special tax regime for movers
Greece

Non-dom (Art 5A): 100,000 euros per year flat on foreign income, up to 15 years, requires a 500,000 euro investment. Pensioners (Art 5B): 7 percent flat on foreign income.5

◐ Good PwC, Greece, incentives Verified 2026-06-24
Portugal

IFICI (the NHR successor, from 2024): 20 percent flat on eligible Portuguese employment and professional income, plus some foreign-income exemptions. Old NHR closed to new entrants at end of 2023.6

◐ Good PwC, Portugal, developments Verified 2026-06-24
Spain

Beckham regime (Art 93 LIRPF): 24 percent flat on Spanish employment income up to 600,000 euros, 47 percent above, for the arrival year plus five years.7
